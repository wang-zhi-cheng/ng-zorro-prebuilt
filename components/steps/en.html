Steps

Stepsis a navigation bar that guides users through the steps of a task.

When To Use#

When the task is complicated or has a certain sequence in the series of subtasks, we can decompose it into several steps to make things easier.

Examples

The most basic step bar.

By setting like this:<nz-steps nzSize="small">, you can get a mini version.

By settingnzStartIndexto change starting index of a step component. Be sure to add an offset tonzCurrentas well.

You can use your own custom icons by setting the propertynzIconfornz-step. Old API is still compatible but we strongly suggest the new API.

Cooperate with the content and buttons, to represent the progress of a process.

A simple step bar in the vertical direction.

A simple mini version step bar in the vertical direction.

By usingnzStatusofnz-steps, you can specify the state for current step.

Steps with progress dot style.

You can customize the display for Steps with progress dot style.

API#

<nz-steps>
  <nz-step nzTitle="first step"></nz-step>
  <nz-step nzTitle="second step"></nz-step>
  <nz-step nzTitle="third step"></nz-step>
</nz-steps>
